# Worcester County Swimming Relay Championships 2025

### Date​
Sunday 15th June 2025

### Location
Sandwell Aquatics Centre

### Age Group
9/10s, 11/12s, 13/14s, 15/16s, Open
Masters: 101-139, 140+

### Age
Age as at day of competition

### Closing Date
4th June 2025

Forms to be returned to swimsecretary@swimworcestercounty.org

### Entry Cost
£15 per relay team

### Spectators
£5 all day (under 8's Free of Charge). Card payments accepted. There is a café on site at the centre.

### Warm-up and Start Time
The fixed depth pool (underneath the score board) will be available throughout the competition as a Warm up / Cool down pool.

* AM Session
  * Warm up: 11:30am - 12:15am
  * Start time: 12:20pm
  * 20 minute break
* PM Session
  * Start time: after 2:00pm
  * Est Finish time: 4:00pm

Warm up schedule (both sessions) Competition Pool (3 x 15 mins)
* Warm Up 1: Masters / 17+ (Male and Female)
* Warm Up 2: age 13 -16 (Male and Female)
* Warm Up 3: age 9 -12 (Male and Female)

### Parking
Parking at the Sandwell Aquatics Centre is very restricted - passes will be sent out via email.

Parking for most will be available at the Harry Mitchell Leisure Centre, Broomfield, Smethwick, B67 7DH. Drop off for swimmers at the centre is permitted.

### Swimmers
Please bring a small camping chair for the poolside. (There are limited benches available)

### Lane Draw
Please use the link on the right to see the draw. Lanes for heats and events that have gone straight to final (8 teams or less participating) have been done by a random draw.

### Hosting Clubs
Each club to provide 2 x personnel (not including officials)
